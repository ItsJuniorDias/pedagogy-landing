## Cow.glb

A low-poly cow — this is **"Spot"**, the classic public-domain cow model by
**Keenan Crane**, converted from `.obj` to `.glb`. It ships with no color and no
skeleton, so the game paints it white with black spots (per-vertex colors) and
gives it a small procedural idle. See `three/cowModel.ts`.

Source: [alecjacobson/common-3d-test-models](https://github.com/alecjacobson/common-3d-test-models)
(file `data/spot.obj`). Original by Keenan Crane — released to the **public domain**.

Suggested credit line to ship in-app:

> Cow model — "Spot" by Keenan Crane (public domain).
